ADAS

Advanced Driver Assistance System (ADAS) are Advanced Technology
Solutions intended to enhance the road safety by preventing or reducing the
impact of potential accidents. ADAS systems activate safety measures by
alerting the driver and initiating possible safeguard measures in the vehicle.
ADAS by Tata Motors is an intuitive driving technology which is built to take
on any surprises while driving, so that you can experience a safe journey with
your loved ones*.
